FIXING BUGS

Find a bug in the app? Create a new ticket in our Issue Tracker! Creating and commenting bugs is free! We will try to fix the bug as soon as possible. To help us prioritize our work, users are able to upvote bugs (by signifying ‘I have this too’).

ADDING FEATURES

To upvote a feature request, users need to donate some money (with a minimum amount of 50Euro) to pay for our time in working on it. In turn, we promise always to spend at least 50% of our time working on developing the features.
